Exemplos

Vocabulário

Presente | Present tense, habits, and general truths. Uses the same conjugation for você and ele/ela.
Pretérito Perfeito | Completed actions in the past at a specific time. Answers the question: "What happened?".
Pretérito Imperfeito | Habits, backgrounds, or ongoing past actions without a specific end. Equivalent to "used to" or "was/were -ing".
Futuro do Presente | Future tense. In spoken pt-br, the structure ir + infinitive (e.g., vou falar) is most commonly used instead.
